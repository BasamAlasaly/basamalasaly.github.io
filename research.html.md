# Research Page - Publications & Acknowledgements

## Page Overview
This page lists academic research, publications, presentations, and scholarly work. It features a filterable list of items and a separate section for acknowledgements and extra-curricular activities.

## Sections

### Publications
- **Filterable List**: Papers, Presentations, Abstracts, Capstones.
- **Filtering**: By type (Paper, Presentation) and Year.
- **Card Design**: Glassmorphism effect, colored left border indicating type.

### Acknowledgements
- **Purpose**: Lists works where contribution was acknowledged but not as a primary author.
- **Cards**:
  1. **Artificial Intelligence in Outpatient Primary Care** (2025): Acknowledged for reading and providing feedback on the manuscript.
  2. **Medical Devices Digital Threads...** (2021): Acknowledged for supply chain and blockchain contributions.
- **Filtering**: Included in year filters.

### Extra-Curricular Activities
- **Purpose**: Lists volunteer work and other academic contributions.
- **Cards**:
    1. **COVID-19 Urgent Statistics Dashboard** (2020): Volunteer development work.
    2. **LeukApp Technical Improvements** (2020): Technical volunteer work.
- **Filtering**: Included in year filters.

## Design Details
- **Navbar**: Sticky glassmorphism header.
- **Animations**: Fade-in up effect for header.
- **Responsive**: Adapts to mobile with a hamburger menu.
